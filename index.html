Intro

Hello, I'm Mohammad Kabir, a passionate and dedicated Computer Science student currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree at The City College of New York's Grove School of Engineering. With a strong academic record, reflected in my GPA of 3.67, I am committed to expanding my knowledge and skills in the field of computer science.

I have a solid foundation in programming, with experience in languages such as Python, C++, JavaScript, and HTML. My technical skill set includes proficiency in frameworks and tools like React, Node.js, MongoDB, SQL, Tailwind CSS, and Bootstrap. I am also well-versed in utilizing development tools such as GitHub and Firebase to streamline collaboration and deployment processes.

My hands-on experience includes developing a C++ implementation of Conway's Game of Life, creating a Python-based reminder application with a comprehensive UI, and working as a Full Stack Web Developer for A. K. Azad, CPA, PLLC. During my internship at STEMKasa, I translated UI/UX designs into functional code and managed sensitive user data, enhancing user experience on a learning platform.

Currently, I am working with ConEdison as a Computer Aide Temp, where I collaborate with the Electric Networks team to improve data management and efficiency. I also have experience in customer service, having worked at Macy's, where I honed my communication and problem-solving skills.

In addition to my technical abilities, I bring strong leadership, critical thinking, and professionalism to every project. I am fluent in Bangla and have conversational proficiency in Hindi and Urdu. I am always eager to learn new technologies and take on challenging projects that allow me to apply my skills and knowledge.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or explore my projects on GitHub, which you will find in the contacts page. Let's create something amazing together!
